What We Offer





Fee Based Ongoing Financial Planning (Business and Personal)

Personal:
We look at all the pieces of your finances and figure out a way to make them work in harmony while addressing your concerns and helping you achieve your financial goals. Our planning process not only incorporates the typical goals like retirement and financial security, but it also integrates exciting life goals - hobbies, trips that you always wanted to take, bucket list etc. Financial planning gives our team the insight that allows us to help you make the right decisions and avoid costly mistakes.

Business:
Just like personal financial planning, we take the same approach and address issues pertaining to business continuation, succession planning, business valuation, employee retention, group benefits, etc. We analyze your business to see how it can translate into supporting your individual needs and goals..





Wealth Management

Wealth management starts by understanding what’s coming in, what’s going out and what’s left. We work closely with you to make sure that you not only have a well-diversified asset, but you also have adequate strategies in place to protect it under different and ever-changing economic environment. We collaborate with your CPA to create financial infrastructures that will provide you with options to optimize the tax consequences efficiently. We also work with your attorney to address estate planning issues to help preserve your wealth the way you want to.





Investment Services

Based on your goals, timeline and risk tolerance, we work with you to create a custom portfolio where we integrate your existing holdings into a unified and appropriately diversified portfolio all while managing investment risks involved. We believe in only taking risks that are necessary to achieve your goals.

Our investment philosophy is based on following the true and proven fundamentals. Our investment fees are structured such that our goals are aligned with yours. In addition to maintaining your investments over time and across market conditions, we also help you remain informed and in charge during the tough times





Insurance and Protection

The foundation of every sound financial plan is to make sure that you protect what you have and build upon it. Based on your goals, we will analyze your needs and develop multiple solutions from different providers for you to choose from. Either it be a simple term life insurance to protect your loved ones or incorporating advanced strategies to complement your estate planning, we can find the right solutions for all your goals.





Retirement

For most people, retirement is one of life’s major goals. Based on when you want to retire, we will make sure what you are doing today is aligned with your retirement goals. We will work with you very closely to come up with a plan that addresses all aspect of your finances (cash flow, savings, investments, tax planning to name a few) with one unified goal of retirement.





Estate Planning

Many people don’t realize how important Estate planning is. We will work closely with an attorney to make sure that your wealth is being used just the way you want it to take care of what is important to you. There is a level of comfort in knowing that everything is going to be taken care of the way you want it to be when you are gone or no longer able to do so.





Tax Planning

Tax is an integral part of Financial Planning. Having assets that have different tax characteristics will provide us with options to deal with unforeseen regulatory futures. We will work with your CPA to make sure that we are proactively integrating tax efficient strategies as we work towards your goals.
