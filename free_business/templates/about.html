About Us

ABOUT ME

We Make Your Ideas
Become True

Hi, I am Saz. DOER Financial Planning is a product of my desire to create a relationship where I can truly be who I am with my client. Its a relationship where I can be objective and brutally honest with what my clients need rather than be driven by product sales. The fact that I am being paid a fee for my time and advise, allows me to understand and recommend appropriate solutions based on my clients goals and removes compensation as a factor for my decisions.

What I do for my clients isnt rocket science. Its very simple actually. Everyone is doing something to secure their financial future. Either it be savings account, 401k or ROTH IRA, we are all trying to do something towards that coveted retirement. The fact that you are still reading this implies that you are serious about doing whats best for you and your family. The way I add value to this equation is by helping you come up with an overall game plan that brings each pieces of your finances together while prioritizing your most important goals and life events along the way.

Philosophy

I believe change is a part of life. Its irrational to expect to get somewhere when you do not know where you are going - intentionality matters. Having a clear and measurable goal backed by a written and actionable plan is the foundation. I believe in creating a focused financial plan that has enough elasticity to pivot and adapt to ever-changing circumstances without ever having to compromise on your goals.

DOER experience

When it comes to finances and your life goals, exceptional service is the norm. At DOER, we cant promise you that our journey will be easy or smooth. There might even be times where you may not like everything we have to say, which is ok. Just know that we will always have your best interest in mind. This is going to be challenging and a lot of work, but we will get through it together.

Our commitment

Action speaks louder at DOER. Together we will get things done or we will not be working together. Action is what ultimately matters if you want to change or achieve anything. We will work with you, for you and keep you accountable for doing the things that you said were important to you. We will work by your side as your advocate and your fiduciary.
